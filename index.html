Vatsak is the largest retail chain of sweets in Europe

"Vatsak Confectionery House" began its journey on March 1, 2001, from a family's passion for sweets. It started as a small workshop in the cozy town of Mogiliv-Podilskiy in the Vinnytsia region.

As of January 1, 2024, we have 995 stores in Ukraine, Romania, Poland, Slovakia, and Moldova. We produce 50,000 sweet products daily, delivered by our own logistics company with 140 Mercedes Benz refrigerated vehicles. Our products can be found in all major national retail chains in Ukraine, Germany, Italy, Spain, and America.
